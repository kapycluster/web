Putting the ease in Kubernetes.

Kapy runs your control planes.

You bring the workers.

We manage the control plane.

Kubernetes is hard. And the control plane is the hardest part. Upgrades, backups, and monitoring are all a pain. Kapy takes care of all that for you.

Your part? The workers.

Kapy manages Kubernetes, but not all of it—just the hard part: the control planes. The nodes can be anything of your choosing. Bare metal, VMs, or even your laptop.

Perfect for homelabs. Inexpensive for enterprise.

Individuals get a free control plane, which makes it perfect for homelabbing. Simply run your services without dealing with the ops. Enterprise customers get a managed control plane for a fraction of the cost of other managed Kubernetes services.
